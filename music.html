Traditional Dances

  • Mokhibo: A women's dance performed kneeling, using graceful arm and shoulder movements to music and chants.
  • Mohobelo: A male-only dance showcasing stamping, jumping, and traditional chants. It expresses strength and unity.

Instruments

  • Lesiba: A stringed wind instrument played by blowing into a string tied to a flexible stick. Unique to the Basotho.
  • Thomo: A mouth bow instrument used by women to produce rhythmic and melodic sounds.
  • Setolo-tolo: A jaw harp-like instrument that mimics vocal rhythms using metal and oral resonance.
